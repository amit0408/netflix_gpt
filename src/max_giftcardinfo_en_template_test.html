Buy a Gift Card online.

You can now easily order a Gift Card online and have it delivered as you would any other product.

Here's what you need to do:

  1. Hit Buy a Gift Card and you’ll go to a page with Gift Card options.
  2. Select your preferred amount and the number of cards you need. If you wish, you can split your amount over multiple cards.
  3. Checkout by adding the Gift Card to your basket or with one-click using our Buy Now feature.
  4. That's it! Your Gift Card will be dispatched to your door.

Shop with your Gift Card online.

Pay with your Gift Card either online, on our app or in-store.
Here's how to use it online:

  1. Find a product you like and proceed to checkout.
  2. At the bottom of your Basket page, enter your 25-digit Gift Card number and 4-digit pin and hit Apply. This will allow you to see how much money you can spend from your Gift Card.
  3. Click on Checkout Now and complete your shopping. It’s that easy!
